About EaseUp

Stress, anxiety, and physical inactivity are quietly becoming public health threats. From packed schedules and constant screen time to sedentary work habits, many people are struggling to care for their mental and physical well-being. EaseUp was created to offer a simple, sustainable way to counter this, helping people feel better through short, accessible practices that fit into daily life.

The facts:

  • 1 in 4 U.S. adults reports getting no physical activity outside of work. This sedentary lifestyle is directly linked to chronic pain, cardiovascular disease, and even early death.
    (Source: America's Health Rankings)
  • Physical inactivity is projected to cause 500 million new cases of preventable disease by 2030, costing the world $27 billion every year.
    (Source: The Lancet Global Health, 2022)
  • More than 1 in 3 U.S. adults reports symptoms of anxiety or depression, yet the majority do not receive professional care.
    (Source: CDC, 2023)
  • Just 10 minutes of daily movement can reduce poor mental health days by over 40%.
    (Source: UCLA Health)

Despite these numbers, most wellness apps feel like another chore. They demand your time, attention, or data.

EaseUp delivers a refreshingly intuitive solution. Quick, meaningful well-being support that fits into real life.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about EaseUp

What is EaseUp?

EaseUp is an intuitive and effective well-being app designed to help users feel better quickly and sustainably through short, actionable practices. Combining guided breathing, mood tracking, reflective journaling, and mobility-based movement routines, EaseUp supports both mental clarity and physical comfort in just minutes a day.

With personalized recommendations, an ad-free experience, a privacy-first design, and no need for an internet connection to use daily features, the app makes self-care effortless and dependable. Whether you need a quick moment of calm, a flexible body, or a clearer mind, EaseUp offers a straightforward path to better well-being.

What's included in a EaseUp subscription?

An EaseUp subscription unlocks the full experience of the app, giving you access to all premium content and features designed to support your mental and physical well-being. The subscription includes:

  • Full access to all breathing exercises, mobility routines, and guided reflections
  • Personalized recommendations based on your goals and usage
  • Premium mood tracking tools with deeper insights over time
  • Early access to new features and exclusive content updates
  • A completely ad-free experience with offline availability for all core features

With a subscription, you get everything you need to make self-care simple, consistent, and effective right from your phone.

Where should I get started once I download the app?

We recommend starting with a quick mood check-in or a guided breathing session. These take just a minute and help you get a feel for the app. You can then explore the four main features — Mood, Breathing, Reflections, and Move — at your own pace.

Each one is designed to support your well-being in a specific way, and you don't need to commit to a long routine to feel the benefits. Just a few minutes a day can make a difference.

What devices support the EaseUp app?

EaseUp is currently available for iPhone running iOS 15 or later. The app is designed and optimized specifically for iPhone to ensure a smooth, focused, and private experience. iPad support is not available at this time.
